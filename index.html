Castlevania97
Instructions
Castlevania97 is a javascript 2D fighting game rendered with HTML canvas inspired by arcade classics like KOF97 and Castlevania.



Player 1 uses W to Jump, A and D to move left and right and S to attack.


Player 2 uses UpArrow to Jump, LeftArrow and RightArrow to move left and right and DownArrow to attack.



Press "Play" to play the music or the press "Insert Coin" to restart.
